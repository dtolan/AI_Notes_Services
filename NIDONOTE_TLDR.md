## FAQ - Common Questions

**Q: How do you make money if guests are free?**
A: The host/manager pays for premium features. Guests join free (up to tier limits: 1 free guest on Essential, 5 on Premium, unlimited on Family tier). Similar to Zoom's model.

**Q: What if I don't trust cloud storage either?**
A: Future roadmap includes end-to-end encryption option where content is encrypted before uploading to your cloud. Only you have the key.

**Q: Why Flutter instead of React Native?**
A: Better Android performance (our priority platform), faster solo development, excellent AI assistant support for Flutter/Dart.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Your data is already in YOUR cloud storage in standard JSON format. You can download it anytime directly from Google Drive/iCloud.

**Q: What happens if NIDOnote shuts down?**
A: Your data is safe in your cloud storage. You'd lose sync functionality but keep all your data. We'll also open-source the sync engine.

**Q: How do you handle conflicts when two people edit the same note?**
A: We use Yjs (CRDT - Conflict-free Replicated Data Type) which automatically merges edits without conflicts. Like Google Docs collaborative editing.

**Q: Is this open source?**
A: Not initially, but we're considering open-sourcing the sync engine and potentially the full app once we validate the business model.

**Q: What about family sharing limits (iCloud Family Sharing, Google Family)?**
A: We leverage native family sharing when available, but NIDOnote's "family" is more flexible - can include friends, extended family, anyone you want to share with.
